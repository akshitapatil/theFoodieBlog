Welcome to theFoodieBlog

Hello Foodies! Hope you all are doing great!

This is Akshita and I welcome you to my new food blog where I confess my weaknesses for food! The white space in my head is always thinking about food. If I’m eating breakfast, I’m already thinking of dinner !

You are at the right stop on your droolworthy journey for food! I happily share my passion for all things salted; sweet and savory, in hope that you too will find pleasure, joy and sustenance in good food. I am going to be sharing my various experiences with different places and their numerous kinds of cuisines in this blog. I am sure your tastebuds are gonna be tempted as you read my reviews and feast on the appetizing images with your eyes.

Also, I promise, only honest reviews here!
